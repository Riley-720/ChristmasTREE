Global Festivities

Christmas is celebrated differently across the globe. Here is a look at some unique traditions:

  1. Germany
    • Christkindlesmarkt: Famous open-air Christmas markets.
    • The Christmas Pickle: An ornament hidden in the tree; the finder gets a gift.
  2. United Kingdom
    • Christmas Pudding: A dense dried-fruit cake often soaked in brandy.
    • Boxing Day: Celebrated on December 26th.
  3. United States
    • Elf on the Shelf: A scout elf that watches behavior for Santa.
    • Stockings: Hung by the chimney with care.
